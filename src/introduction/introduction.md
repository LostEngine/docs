## What is LostEngine?

LostEngine is a custom item plugin whose particularity is that it creates new items 
on the server-side like Fabric and Forge mods do but on Paper and Folia servers.
This allows having a great compatibility with plugins and way better performance 
than other custom item plugin because nothing has to be after that your server has booted
except for editing packets sent to your players.
